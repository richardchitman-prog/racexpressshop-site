Reporting Agencies

Three Agencies. Maximum Impact.

Each agency has different enforcement powers. Reporting to all three puts maximum pressure on scammers.

🏛️
FTC
Federal Trade Commission
The FTC maintains the National Do Not Call Registry and takes enforcement action against telemarketers who violate consumer protection laws.
✓ Can issue fines up to $51,744 per violation
📡
FCC
Federal Communications Commission
The FCC regulates communications networks and can take action against carriers that allow illegal robocall traffic, imposing fines up to $10,000 per call.
✓ Can shut down illegal calling operations
🔍
FBI/IC3
Internet Crime Complaint Center
For scam calls involving fraud or identity theft, IC3 collects and refers complaints to federal, state, and local law enforcement agencies for investigation.
✓ Refers cases for criminal prosecution

RoboCallMeNot helps you report robocalls to the agencies that can take enforcement action. It doesn't block calls at the carrier level — it helps you fight back through official channels.
